about heading

Join us on a walking tour

Operating for over 50 years, 60 Minute Soho is a nonprofit cultural institution that seeks to preserve Sohos history and the stories that make it the vibrant district that it is today.


All content of our walking tours has been compiled by local historians, using evidence from historical records and first-hand recollections in order to tailor authentic and meticulous stories.


tours heading

Tours on Now:
Soho Sound

Starting on Carnaby Street, you're taken on a whilstestop tour of Sohos famous music venues as our guide delivers stories about the people who shaped music from the 50s til the present day.

Beating Art

Journey around the streets to discover sohos vibrant and evolving street-art scene and learn about the artists that call Soho home.

Building Soho

Discover the districts diverse mix of architectural styles and learn how Soho went from fields to a melting pot of homes and workshops.
